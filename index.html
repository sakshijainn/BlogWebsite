Can you relate to the Light of a Star?

But as much I know journey? every letter of this word is build with a lot of patience and hardships. Do we ever wonder the shine we see is more of the part of its dedication to stay constant in its journey. Yes I am talking about a star. Pause…….

Friendship-Beautiful Word?


So, heard about friendship a lot! A very dreamy word, or an imaginary tie that everyone wants to have with someone in this life!The tie that understands you,loves you and cares for you endlessly!Do such thing exists? If yes,then “beautiful” is another word for ” friendship”.

So all this was the theoritical crap everyone wants to feel. Giving in a lot to the bond is all we learned about the friendship! Who says we don’t expect in friendship?whoever says actually lies to himself.

Obviously giving in so much would expect something in return. In return? What ?

The same love, affection, attention and honesty!

Now I can say if this overall tedious and technical process of tie exists- ” Friendship is beautiful”

Nothing is constant. No promise, no commitments! But irony is we always want our bond to be constant!

Believe me, saying anyone that I am there for you, no matter what sucks! Yet a very beautiful line to impress but most misinterpreted line so far.

So this beautiful word somewhere has circle of vicious words revolving around it. “Expectations”,”Insecurity” , ” Fear of loosing” and so on!

Our grip do have a handful of beautiful friendship but the vicious circle loosens up that grip and “beautiful” thing slips down like sand.

Yet now, I will say Friendship a beautiful word? !Think deep.
